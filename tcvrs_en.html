Thai CV 2015

The Risk Estimator is intended as a companion tool to estimate 10-year risk for atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease, defined as coronary death or nonfatal myocardial infarction, or fatal or nonfatal stroke, based on EGAT study, pooled 20-year Cohort study. This Estimator could be use without blood testing, required waist circumference and height, or use with blood testing. However this risk estimator should be apply only in 35-70 year-old Thai population without previous atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease. Please contact your personal care provider if you have question.
